Physical & Wellness Benefits at Prairie View A&M University

Empowering students to thrive physically, mentally, and socially through campus wellness programs and resources.

Fitness Mental Health Nutrition Recreation

Fitness & Athletics

PVAMU offers a state of the art Recreation Center with cardio equipment, weight training areas, and group fitness classes. Whether you’re training for athletics or simply staying active, there’s something for everyone. Students can join intramural sports or fitness programs that promote healthy lifestyles and teamwork.

Mental Health & Wellness

PVAMU’s Counseling Services provide confidential support to help students manage stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges. Programs like meditation sessions, mental wellness workshops, and peer support groups help you stay balanced and focused.

Nutrition & Healthy Eating

Healthy eating is made easy with campus dining options offering balanced meals and vegetarian choices. PVAMU promotes nutrition awareness through events and educational programs so students can make informed, healthy choices every day.

Recreation & Outdoor Activities

Take advantage of outdoor recreation fields, walking trails, and sports leagues. Whether you’re playing basketball, swimming, or exploring campus grounds, PVAMU encourages students to stay active and engaged in community wellness.
