Your Path to Financial Aid

The Prairie View A&M University Office of Student Financial Aid and Scholarships is here to help you navigate the process of funding your education. The first step is always completing the FAFSA.

1. Types of Financial Aid

Grants (Must Not Repay)

  • Federal Pell Grant
  • FSEOG
  • TEXAS Grant (State)
  • TPEG (Texas Public Education Grant)

Loans (Repayable)

  • Federal Direct Subsidized Loan
  • Federal Direct Unsubsidized Loan
  • Federal Direct PLUS Loan (Parent/Grad)
  • Short-Term Loans (Treasury Services)

Federal Work-Study

Allows students to earn money for educational expenses through part-time employment on or off campus. Great for gaining work experience!

View Student Employment

Exemptions/Waivers

Financial assistance for certain Texas residents (e.g., Hazlewood, Blind/Deaf Students, Foster Care alumni) that waives all or part of tuition/fees.

Learn More

2. Scholarships & Key Resources

Institutional & Merit-Based

PVAMU offers highly competitive scholarships based on academic achievement.

  • **Regents' Student Merit Scholarship** (Most prestigious, covers tuition, housing, meals, books)
  • Presidential Academic Scholarship
  • Transfer and Departmental Scholarships
  • General Scholarship Application required annually.

Other Key Financial Resources

  • **Student Emergency Assistance Fund (SEAF):** For students facing financial hardships that might prevent graduation.
  • **Financial Literacy:** The Office of Financial Aid provides educational resources to manage money.
  • **Panther Meal Share Program:** To address food insecurity on campus.
  • **Career Closet:** Provides professional attire for interviews and career fairs.
